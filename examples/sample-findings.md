# Sample Findings

## Finding: Privileged Role Review Not Current

- Severity: Medium
- Area: Identity and access management
- Observation: Privileged role assignments have not been formally reviewed in the last quarter.
- Risk: Excessive access may persist after role changes or project completion.
- Recommendation: Implement a quarterly privileged access review with evidence retention and accountable owners.

## Finding: Storage Account Allows Public Network Access

- Severity: Medium
- Area: Data protection
- Observation: A storage account permits public network access without documented business justification.
- Risk: Misconfiguration may increase exposure of sensitive data or services.
- Recommendation: Restrict network access, confirm application dependencies, and record approved exceptions.
